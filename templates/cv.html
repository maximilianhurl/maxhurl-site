Experience

DabApps Ltd - Engineer

July 2012 - Present

I joined as a graduate after university with limited Python experience (the core language used at the agency) and picked up the skills needed to produce my first web app within a few months (Twilbee). Within 12 months I had gained enough skills and trust to be technical lead on a small scale hybrid app and REST backend (https://www.travelplaces.mobi/).

I also took the lead on a hybrid angular app and website (Biteappy). For this project I worked with a new graduate hire whom I had to take under my wing. The project included a large amount of functionality within a short deadline which we managed to achieve without cutting too many corners. After this project I became the go-to person for supporting new hires.

The largest project I have worked on is TootCompare (now rebranded). I started this site as a junior developer but as the site and my skills grew I became the project lead. In this role I instigated and implemented a gradual move to a service orientated API driven architecture which allowed us to easily produce several white label sites (BookMyGarage and GoCompare Garage Compare). This allowed a larger team to produce two new sites using more modern technologies without needing to fully understand or amend the legacy code within the core site.
